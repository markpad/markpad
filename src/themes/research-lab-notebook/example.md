## Hypothesis

Increasing buffer pH from 6.8 to 7.2 will reduce variance in response time.

## Procedure

1. Prepare two sample groups of equal size.
2. Run calibration sequence for 15 minutes.
3. Capture values every 5 seconds.

| Group | pH | Mean Response | Std Dev |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Control | 6.8 | 118ms | 21ms |
| Variant | 7.2 | 103ms | 12ms |

> Observation: Variant stabilized faster after warm-up.

### Conclusion

Data supports the hypothesis and recommends pH 7.2 for the next run.
