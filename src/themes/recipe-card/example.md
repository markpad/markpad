# Sunday Pasta

## Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- Fresh basil and parmesan

## Timing

| Step | Duration |
| --- | --- |
| Prep | 10 min |
| Simmer sauce | 20 min |
| Cook pasta | 9 min |

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil and cook garlic for 1 minute.
2. Add tomatoes, simmer until thickened.
3. Toss pasta with sauce and finish with basil.

> Reserve half a cup of pasta water to adjust consistency before serving.
