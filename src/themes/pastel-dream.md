# ✨ Welcome to Pastel Dream

_Where colors dance and creativity blooms_ 🌸

## A Gentle Aesthetic

Pastel Dream brings a **soft, dreamy** atmosphere to your content. Perfect for creative portfolios, lifestyle blogs, and anything that needs a touch of whimsy.

### Why Pastels?

Pastel colors are known for their:

- 💜 Calming effect
- 💗 Friendly appearance
- 💙 Creative versatility
- 💚 Modern appeal

> "Colors are the smiles of nature."  
> — Leigh Hunt
